# Tensura:

## Plot Summary
Rimuru Tempest, once an ordinary man, is reborn as a slime in a fantasy world.  
He gains unique powers, befriends monsters, and builds a nation where all races can live in peace.

---

### Key Themes
- **Friendship & Unity**  
- **Nation-building**  
- **Transformation & Identity**  
- **Power & Responsibility**

---

## Main Characters
- **Rimuru Tempest** – Protagonist, slime turned Demon Lord  
- **Shizu** – Mentor with a tragic past  
- **Benimaru** – Fiery general  
- **Shion** – Loyal bodyguard  
- **Veldora** – Storm Dragon, Rimuru’s ally
